Welcome to Texas State Parking!

Problem

Students are struggling to find parking at TXST which greatly limits productivity as well as causes unexpected tardiness. The forces, which are out of students' control, increases the stress unnecessarily and risks credit failure in some cases.

Group Members

Sam Roberts : Design Research, UI/UX
Sean Tibbetts : Functionality, UI/UX
Adrian Flores : Wireframing, UI/UX

Who

We’re targeting Texas State University students who struggle with parking. Our app helps students quickly find spots and offers valet services, aiming to reduce stress and improve campus mobility.

What

Our project is an innovative parking app for Texas State University, offering real-time availability and valet services. It’s designed to enhance campus mobility and provide a smoother parking experience for users.

Why

We’re solving Texas State’s parking challenges by providing a user-friendly app that saves time, reduces frustration, and offers convenience, making campus life easier for students, faculty, and visitors.
