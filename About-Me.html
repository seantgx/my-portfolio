About Me:

Hey there! I am Sean, and I am a graphic designer by trade focused on branding, web development, motion graphics, and more. My design journey began as a kid making some hilariously bad YouTube thumbnails, but it sparked a lifelong passion in design and identity. I generally prefer brighter designs although I have diverse branding experience working with both corporations and startups.
When I’m not designing, you’ll find me rock climbing (since I was 14) or training advanced calisthenics moves. These pursuits push me mentally and physically, fueling the creativity I bring to my work. I’m always up for a challenge, and whether it’s in design or athletics, I dive in fully.
Reach out for creative projects, collaborations, or general business!
